The passing of Walser Janeway, our eldest Twilighter

Walser Janeway died on February 8th after a long battle with Parkinson’s Disease just after his 94th birthday.
He left us quietly Tuesday at noon after bidding farewell to his beloved wife Ann and treasured dog, Jag.
Walser was born February 1st, 1917 on Staten Island, NY to Dr. William Richard Janeway and Carolyn Rodman Janeway.
He was educated at South Kent School and Princeton University.
He left college to pursue work and was soon inducted into Army Air Force flight school.
He flew many missions from Foggia, Italy, returning home after the World War II to raise his family of four boys as an Air Force,
then Pratt and Whitney Aircraft employee.
He loved his friends and family and his Twilight Park summers.

A time of remembrance and celebration is being planned for August in Twilight Park.
