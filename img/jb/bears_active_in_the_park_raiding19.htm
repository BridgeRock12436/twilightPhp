Bears active in the Park raiding garbage sheds...Note by Superintendent

 

Last night (weds night)  a few cottagers garbage sheds were torn apart by bears. Please use extra
care leaving garbage out until the pick up days. Chardie Hay is printing DEC guidelines on preventive
measures for bears which should be in the mailboxes by the end of the weekend.
If anyone has any bear problems, please call me at 589-6483. After hours and weekends, calls to that
number will forwarded to my cell phone.

Nathan
