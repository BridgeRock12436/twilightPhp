2009 All Angels Benefit - A Cabaret

To My Fellow Twilighters and Drama Queens:

The Auxiliary of All Angels is pleased to announce that its Annual Benefit will be held on Saturday, August 1st.
We will be organizing and producing an Evening Cabaret, an event which has been successful (and a great deal of fun!) in years past.
Mack Elder will supply the book, and Katrina Swanson Pulichene will serve as Stage Manager and Choreographer !

This Theme, for 2009: The Music of the Beatles!!!

So first, I must:

1) Try to determine just how many Twilighters, aged 16 or older, might be interested in participating in the Cabaret; and

2) Poll all those interested: What particular Beatles tune are you drawn to, tunes you would like to perform individually or in partnership with other Twilighters?

In this way, we can begin to envision the scope of the show, the flow, the number of singers, gather up sheet music, etc.
