Changes in the Twilight Board

Dear Cottagers,

            It is with real regret that I announce that Fernando Viana has retired from the Board.
He was only a few months short of two full terms, but the birth of his daughter Tessa, and
some new consulting work have led him to want to reclaim some of his time for himself. Fernando
has been very generous with his time, and has offered highly valued counsel to me, Peter Swords
 and Bob Elder. I have very much enjoyed working with Fernando, relied on his meticulous
analytical approach, and wish to thank him for the thorough and wholehearted way in which he
 fulfilled his Board service.

             We are very fortunate that Allan Lans has agreed to take Fernando's place on the Board.
Allan began his career as an M.D. and had a family practice for about twenty years, before becoming
a psychiatrist. In that capacity he has been the Clinical Director of the Smithers Institute, the resident
psychiatrist for the New York Mets, is now an Assistant Clinical Professor of Psychiatry at Columbia,
 and has a private practice. It is anticipated that he will bring humanistic and interpersonal skills to the
Board which we have not seen since Socrates was a member! We very much look forward to welcoming
him at the May meeting.

                                                                    Joe Keiffer
