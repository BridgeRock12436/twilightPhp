Dear Fellow Cottagers,


      I am told that Twilighters who have chosen to accept Onteora's "Dining Membership" have been using the lake at Onteora.
"Dining Membership" at Onteora entitles one only to use the Field House for meals; this membership does not entitle Twilighters to
use any other Onteora facility, including the lake. It would be a shame if this amenity for us all were withdrawn due to abuse by a few.
Please be as respectful of the Onteora rules as we would expect them to be of ours.


Many thanks!
Joe Kieffer
