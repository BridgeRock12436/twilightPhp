Joyce Meyer Passed Away 12.05.09





      It is with great sadness that we would like to tell you that Joyce Ann Meyer died early on the morning of Saturday, December 5th, 2009.

      Joyce ended her year-long struggle with lung cancer in peace, surrounded by her family and loved ones at her home in Twilight Park.

      Thank you all for the many gifts, well wishes and prayers that we received throughout this last year.
      Joyce was comforted by the knowledge that she had so many dear friends who were pulling for her.

      Joyce's final wishes were to be cremated and buried in the garden at All Angels Church, in Twilight Park.
      We will be planning a Memorial Service at All Angels Church in the Summer of 2010. More information to follow.


      Joyce is survived by her husband Bernhard, and children Christoph, Jane and Rob. We will miss her very, very much.


      In lieu of food or flowers please consider a donation to The Community Hospice of Columbia/Green County.
      Please visit the funeral home website soon, for details on how to donate and to view a copy of Joyce's Obituary. Decker Funeral Home



      Love,

      The Meyer Family
