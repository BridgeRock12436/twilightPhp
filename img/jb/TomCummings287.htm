Tom Cummings passed away on Oct 13,2010 at his home in Vero Beach surrounded by his loving family.
There will have a memorial service and a reception this summer in Twilight Park.
Dates and times will be announced when plans are confirmed.
Below is the obituary published in the NY Times and a longer one can be found at the link below

CUMMINGS--Robert Thomas (Tom), 88, died on October 13, 2010 at his home in Vero Beach, Florida.
He retired as President of the Fibers Division of AlliedSignal in 1984.
Tom was known by all for his intellect, unbelievable sense of humor, generosity, and love of children.
He is survived by his wife Carol Cummings, three daughters, Sarah Post, Katie Cummings and Christine Cummings, and two stepdaughters Lynn Corson and Lisa Corson.
