TWILIGHT PARK

COTTAGES FOR RENT

2009

 

 

AINSWORTH – On quiet Church Lane between first and second Level.  Lots of original wainscoting and a large charming dining room. 4 bedrooms on second floor, 1 bedroom and playroom on third. 3 and ½ baths. Plenty of nooks, crannies and porches. View of Kaaterskill Falls and the Clove.

 

AULT –Well maintained, lovely home with wainscoting, diamond paned windows, in living and sitting room; stained glass windows in dining room,  newer kitchen, large deck and covered porch.  4 bedrooms; 1 and ½ baths. Second Level road, near Pebble Rock Road, halfway between pool and tennis courts. Woodland view.

 

BARISH/STERRETT – Among the first cluster of cottages built in Twilight, this pretty cottage has lots of wainscoting and golden birch accents. Brand new kitchen and bathroom with stall shower on first floor, 4 bedrooms and bath on second. “Sit on the covered porch overlooking a spacious gently sloping lawn and listen to Haines Falls. Located halfway up Pharmacy Hill Road Woodland and lawn view.

 

BEHLKE – “Far View” cottage, built by the founder of Twilight Park to be reminiscent of a Japanese cottage. Set back from the road; surrounded by the remnants of a once cultivated Japanese garden. Comfortable living room, inviting outside porches. 5 bedrooms; 3 full baths. Second Level, near All Angels Church. Woodland view.

 

COBB – Stately Victorian.  Large living room with brand new half bath and a moose head over the fireplace. Newer kitchen.Expansive covered wrap-around porch; sunny deck off of kitchen. Grand staircase with a landing leads to second floor with 5 bedrooms; 2 full baths. Newly renovated third floor TV room provides two additional bedrooms. Basement: one bedroom apartment with kitchenette/sitting TV room. Set back from Pharmacy Hill Road. Woodland view. No pets.

 

J.DAYTON – Totally renovated cottage with spectacular clove view. Cottage on 3 floors. First floor has new kitchen, dining room with fireplace, powder room and a deck with a fireplace. Second floor has living room, covered porch, master bedroom and bath. Third floor has two bedrooms and two baths. Haywood Road off of First Level road. Clove view.

 

DECKER  - Large, stately home with 6 bedrooms; 4 baths. First floor has open floor plan of living room/dining area with stone fireplace and diamond paned windows. French doors lead to patio and expansive lawn and rhododendron garden. Large kitchen has adjacent bath with shower. Upstairs features include two floors of bedrooms, some with accesses to covered porch. 3 baths, a large sitting room and a master suite. Second Level location, across from All Angels Chapel, adjacent to Wingate field. Woodland view. No animals.

 

HIRSCH –This pleasing cottage offers a panoramic view of Prospect Mountain to be enjoyed especially on the covered porch and in the paneled living room with large stone fireplace. There is a fully equipped 50’s kitchen. and two bedrooms and bath on the first floor. More bedrooms and bath upstairs. Located on quiet Third Level. Well-landscaped grounds and level land. Mountain view.

 

JANEWAY – The cottage of one of the founding families of Twilight, this cottage is nostalgic of times gone by. Adjacent to the living room is a window-seated music and game room complete with a workable upright Steinway piano! Dining room, kitchen, laundry and full bath on first floor, 4 bedrooms and bath with claw foot tub on second. Wrap around porch with deck extension to catch the sun. Driveway leads directly to the deck. Second Level. Woodland and rock view.

 

MACK –A welcoming cottage, features a large eat-in kitchen with half bath leading to a sun-filled deck in the back. Level property and playhouse is great for children and grandchildren. 1 bedroom on ground floor, 3 bedrooms, sleeping nook and bath on second floor, 1 bedroom, playroom, and bath with shower in basement. Second Level. Walk through the woods to access pool and Wingate Field. Woodland view, clove view from master bedroom’s diamond paned windows.

 

SPITKO – A comfy cottage with wainscoting and golden birch inside; covered porch on rear of cottage. Stained glass windows in living room and dining room. 3 bedrooms, 1 and ½ baths plus a separate shower. Second Level road near Pebble Rock, halfway between pool and tennis courts. Woodland view. Winterized. Available for winter rental.

 

VIANA - Situated on the ledge directly opposite Kaaterskill Falls, this cottage has wide open views of the Falls and Clove towards the Hudson River. Beautifully appointed and maintained cottage with designer eat-in kitchen, living/dining room with stone fireplace, child-proof covered porch and open deck to all views. 4 bedrooms and 2 full baths upstairs and a half-bath downstairs, large washer/dryer in enclosed basement. Landscape artist-designed perennial gardens.  Centrally located on First Level.
