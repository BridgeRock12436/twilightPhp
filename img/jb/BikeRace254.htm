Tour of the Catskills – Pro Am Bicycle Race


On July 30, 31 & August 1st, the third annual Tour of the Catskills road bicycle race will be held on our mountaintop.

Over 500 cyclists are expected to participate, double the number from last year!

Friday’s time trial will feature a 2.3 mile race up 23-C from the Orpheum in Tannersville to All Soul’s Church.

Saturday’s 51 mile race (80 mi. for pros) begins at Windham Mountain and ends in the center of Windham.

Sunday’s race begins at Hunter Mountain and ends in the Village of Hunter at the Catskill Mountain Foundation.
On Sunday, the 57 mile ride will include the infamous ‘Devil’s Kitchen’, (Platte Clove Road).

We encourage you all to participate by volunteering as road marshals or in another volunteer capacity.
For any of you who would like to ride in the race, we have a 40-mile non-competitive ride on Sunday.

To sign-up, go to:  www.bikereg.com
