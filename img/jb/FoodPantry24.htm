Twilighters! Don't Forget The Food Pantry when leaving the Mountain top.
 
 
 

Joyce and Bernie Meyer remind us all that a real contribution to the Mountain Top community can be made by
leaving unopened cans of food and drink for the Food Pantry in Tannersville.

Contact the Meyers at 589- 5960 for easy directions.

It will really help.
