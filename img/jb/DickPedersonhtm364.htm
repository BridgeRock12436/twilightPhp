The passing of Dr. Richard Pedersen

Dr. Richard Pedersen passed away peacefully at home on Monday.
As his friend and fellow cottager the Reverend Fred Burnham wrote, Dick was "a person of peace, moderation and conciliation.
I loved him for his modest byut crystal clear mind, his soft-spoken but discerning spirit and his joy in people and life".

A memorial service celebrating his life will be held at the All Angels Church on Saturday August 6th at 3 pm.
Immediately following the service, there will be a reception at Dick and Nelda's cottage, Moonshine. All are warmly invited to attend.
