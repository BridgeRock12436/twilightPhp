Greg Dayton performing Live at Last Chance Tavern


Singer/songwriter/guitarist Greg Dayton will be performing live at the Last Chance Tavern in Tannersville on Friday, July 16th at 9pm.

Traditional acoustic blues, rootsy folk and classic rock with influences from Van Morrison, Eric Clapton and James Taylor to Muddy Waters and Robert Johnson.

Greg has performed extensively in the US and Europe for over 20 years including recent appearances on NBC's Today Show and New York's Blue Note.

No cover charge required.
