Garden Fair


The Twilight Park Garden Club wants to call your attention to the Garden Fair this Saturday, June 30, at the Mountain Top Arboretum. Hours are from 10 am to 2pm. Admission is free! There will be guided tours and talks on the bee hive, the new vegetable garden, and highlights of the West Meadow. The festivities include an art show by the Windham Arts Alliance. Master Gardeners will also be on hand to answer gardening questions and plants will be available for sale. http://mtarboretum.org/
