WATER NOTICE



            In your mailboxes at the gatehouse there are boil water notices for
twilight. These are strictly precautionary. Our chlorinator acted up (For
the first time this year), at the same time the DOH inspector was here, so
we have to follow the state procedures for a lapse in disinfection. The
chlorinator is now working and there is a chlorine residual throughout the
park. The boil water ban order will be lifted as soon as we get the results
of two tests next week, I'll let you know as soon as I get them.
On a related note, please don't bring dogs go in the upper reservoir as
this is our source of drinking water. the lower reservoir can be used for
any recreational purpose.
