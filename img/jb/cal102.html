See the 2009 Summer\'s Events on the Twilight Website

  The Twilight web calendar, which lives at the Twilight Park website, supplements the hard-copy calendar
that is available in cottagers' boxes each Memorial Day weekend. The web calendar
provides detailed descriptions of Twilight events and is a way to notify cottagers
in addition to word-of-mouth, flyers, and the Owl.


To see the events posted to date, log on to www.twilightpark.com   , click on the tab for 'calendar' (in the top grey bar),
and bring up whatever month that interests you using the drop-down box titled 'month'.
To view the full description of each event, rather than it's summary, just
'mouse over' it (place your mouse over the event summary - but don't click on it).

To see only the events that are listed to a particular category,
select a category under the drop-down box 'category'. Categories include
Park or All, All Angels church, Camp, Clubhouse, Garden Club, Golf,
Linger-nots, Pool, and Tennis.


John Galatti and Nora Monaco manage the calendar, and people with
'permission' to update each separate category are: John and Nora and Bonnie
