Dear Cottagers,




We, Twilight Park, were approached by AT&T with the proposal that they erect
a cell tower on our property. This came at a time when the board was
searching for new sources of income. The Board unanimously voted to explore
the possibility. And we have. As part of this process an attorney was hired
to help us understand what would be involved. That lawyer, respected in the
field and referred by several sources was paid , I believe, $2,000.00 which
would be recouped if the tower were to be erected. The Board has been
neutral on the subject, neither advocating nor dismissing. We feel that this
is an issue that should be brought to all cottagers. Based on the response
of all members of the community an appropriate and wise decision could be
made. No commitments have been made. No other actions have been taken.
Again. the Board remains neutral on this topic. No matter how else it has
been portrayed we are trying to carry forth with the best interests of the
entire community.


It would be irresponsible on the Board's part to not bring this matter to
the entire community to consider.


On the day of our Memorial Day picnic there will be a meeting in the
clubhouse where we invite all to come and ask questions, voice concerns,
express opinions.




Respectfully,


Allan
