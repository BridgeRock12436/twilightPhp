A heads up for everyone who has ever desired to take a class with Gracia Dayton.
This summer, you have a perfect opportunity to do so.

On Saturday and Sunday, July 17 and 18, Gracia will be teaching a two-day workshop in Mixed Media Collages at Sugars Maples Center for the Arts in Maplecrest.

All levels of experience are welcome, ages 15 and up -- 10AM to 4PM each day.

It's what you've always wanted to do in the summer, take a class! Something for yourself.
An opportunity to spend two days focusing on art, learning from Gracia, and enjoying the peace and beauty of the village of Maplecrest.

Sign up for the workshop with friends or family members.
During lunch or after class, you can visit the Sugar Maples Ceramics Studio and view their classes and pottery, stop by the Podell Library and view the 130 exotic objects in the art collection,
or visit the Catskill Mountain Foundations' organic garden.

To read more about Sugar Maples summer classes, and to sign up for Gracia Dayton's workshop please visit --- www.sugarmaples.org
