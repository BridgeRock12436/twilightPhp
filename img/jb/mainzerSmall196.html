We are very sorry to share the news of the death of our Mother Sara Mainzer on 1 April. This summer there will be a memorial
service and internment of ashes at All Angels, followed by a reception. We hope you will be able to join us, and will announce
the date when plans firm up. Below is the obituary that will be published in the Windham Journal. Attached is a photo of Sara
taken in the 1970’s. We also scanned it in a higher resolution if anyone would like to have it.



-- Jim, Janet, and John Mainzer



      Sara M. Mainzer, 93, former long-time Twilight Park cottager, passed away on 1 April 2010 after a short illness. Born in
New York City on 11 November 1916, she was the daughter of John and Josephine McManus, and wife of the late Dr. Robert A. Mainzer.


      Sara earned her B. A. at Randolph Macon College in Ashland Virginia, and was among the first women to earn her Masters in Business
at Columbia University in New York City.
