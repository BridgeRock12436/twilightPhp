Combining The Twilight Web Page with The Twilight Directory

 

My.  I have enjoyed monitoring the Twilight web site for the last eight years. It has been great to get an early view
of Twilight and Mountain Top happenings, births, weddings and Twilight Art Shows as well as a chance to mirror
the Twilight Owls, Snowy Owls, all the important updates from Whitney Janeway and bulletins from the Board and
Superintendents.

Now there is a whole generation here in the Park who have grown up with the Internet and know how to use it and
program it. John and Nora Galatti with their Twilight Directory have shown the many new ways that the Internet
can be used make Twilight work better and connect to the outside world.

The Twilight Web Page at www. twilightpark.com  will be combined with the Twilight Directory but the address will remain
www.twilightpark.com. Most of the material from 2005 through 2008 has been saved and selected pieces from
December 2000 (Y2K)  to 2005 may also be available.

The shifting of the web site and the new combined web site will be available September 24. You will receive
login instructions as a "Twilight Web Page Update" from me and also from John and Nora from their hosting service,
Panther. There are always a few problems in fitting two web sites together but there is no doubt that it will become
a very valuable community tool.

Of course, John, Nora and I will be available to make the transition as smooth as possible.

JBP
