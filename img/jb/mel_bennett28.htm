Mel Bennett's car meets a bear

I had a bear attack last night on my car. The rear windshield was completely smashed in and
significant body damage was incurred. There was no food/drink in my car. Doug from Haines Falls 
Auto believes the bear was attracted to the green cloth recycling bags I use to carry groceries which
I leave in my car. So Twilighters need to be aware!
 
Mel
